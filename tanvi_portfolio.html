About Me

I'm a Computational Associate at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard, specializing in multi-modal single-cell and spatial transcriptomics analysis. My work focuses on understanding complex immune responses in diseases like atopic dermatitis, IBD, and glioblastoma through advanced computational methods.

With a background in bioinformatics and biology from UC San Diego, I develop analytical pipelines and visualization tools to extract biological insights from high-dimensional genomic data.
